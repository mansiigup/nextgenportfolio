The Problem

Dining should be a
happy moment.
Often it isn't.

Six friction points silently ruin the dining experience for millions of people every single day.

📖
Menu Confusion
Menus written in culinary jargon leave diners guessing. No visuals, no context — just unfamiliar words and zero confidence in what they're ordering.
"I just pointed at something and hoped for the best."
🌍
Language Barriers
International travelers face menus entirely in foreign scripts. Even when translated, dish names carry no cultural context — "Rogan Josh" still tells you nothing.
"I ate something I didn't order and couldn't complain."
🎩
Fine Dining Anxiety
High-end menus amplify insecurity. No portion sizes, no plating preview, fear of looking unknowledgeable in front of dates or business clients.
"I just copied what my client ordered."
⏳
Waiting Anxiety
After placing an order, diners enter a black hole. No visibility, no ETA, no way to know if the order was received — mood deteriorates with every passing minute.
"The mood was completely ruined by the time it arrived."
🖼️
Expectation vs. Reality
The dish arrives nothing like imagined. Wrong portion, wrong texture, unexpected ingredients. Too late and too awkward to send it back.
"It had ingredients I don't eat. It was too late."
🔇
Silent Disconnect
No channel between diner and kitchen except a busy waiter. Special requests feel like a burden. Real-time feedback doesn't exist. Problems go unfixed.
"I didn't want to be 'that customer.' So I stayed quiet."

Technology

Built on a foundation
of intelligent layers.

  • Computer Vision & OCR
    Scans physical menus in any condition — handwritten, printed, backlit — and extracts structured dish data in real time.
  • Multimodal LLM Engine
    Enriches raw dish names with descriptions, cultural context, ingredient lists, and preparation methods across all cuisines.
  • Neural Machine Translation
    Goes beyond word translation — delivers culturally accurate, contextually meaningful menu explanations in 50+ languages.
  • Real-Time Kitchen API
    POS-integrated order status feed. Connects restaurant kitchen systems to the diner's device for live tracking.
  • Preference Graph Engine
    Builds a personalized flavor and preference model over time — improving recommendations with every single meal.
